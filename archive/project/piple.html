PiPle

Topics : Project Management, Team Work, Entrepreneurship, development, OpenSource, Business

Introduction

PiPle is the next generation professional communication tool. It is a cross platform project led by a team of Engineering student from ECEparis which is led by Paul Best and myself. We won a couple of Prizes for this project last year. The Berger Levrault Citizen Challenge as well as ECE imagine CUP. We are now in the midst of creating the alpha version of the project. This one will be distributed to different companies during mid 2018. We are now composed of a team of a dozen people working part time on this complex project.
You can find more information about this project and how to help us on the facebook page .

My Involvment

This Project came from an idea I had during the end of my 2nd year, at my first internship. A fundamental problem in in how people communicate both at work and privately made me realise that a new way of communicating was possible. I am now leading this proejct since a year and a half. Together with Paul we focuse more now on managing the project, creating the business plan and the fundraising. You can send me a message on any platform if you want to back, help or work with our team at piple.
